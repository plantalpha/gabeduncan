As I sit and Wonder

by Gabe Duncan (April 29th, 2025)

If I hadn’t known
what I had known before
Then I would be out wrecking
Stealing all the spoils of war
But alas as I was gifted
With a secret deep inside
So instead I stay at home
Wondering why we die

It’s a simple question
But I think that I’ve heard
It isn’t as straight forward
Because death is like a bird
The soul is like the air
That supports its wings below
And we are like the trees
That truly never know

Standing firm on high
And with the strength in fate
Witness the ancient task
With love we captivate
Joy it fills the empty air
No longer can we mourn
For summers are no longer long
The courts have been adjourned

A bird it sits upon my branch
And wonders of my strength
When I am you and you be me
Then nothing will be late
When all is one and you can see
The prevalence of woo
Then we can all be happy
The confluence of You

Gabe Duncan
